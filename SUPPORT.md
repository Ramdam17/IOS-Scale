### Frequently Asked Questions

#### How do I create a measurement session?
1. Open the app and select a measurement modality from the home screen
2. Drag the circles or use the slider to adjust the measurement value
3. Optionally add labels for "Self" and "Other"
4. Tap "Done" to save the measurement

#### How do I export my data?
1. Go to History
2. Select the sessions you want to export
3. Tap the export button
4. Choose your preferred format (CSV, TSV, or JSON)

#### How does iCloud sync work?
When enabled, your measurement sessions automatically sync across all your devices signed in with the same Apple ID. You can enable or disable this in Settings > iCloud Sync.

#### How do I delete my data?
- **Single session**: Swipe left on a session in History and tap Delete
- **All data**: Go to Settings > Reset Data
- **iCloud data**: Disable iCloud Sync in Settings, then delete local data

#### What are the different measurement modalities?
- **Basic IOS**: Classic two-circle overlap (Inclusion of Other in Self)
- **Advanced IOS**: Two-circle overlap with adjustable sizes
- **Overlap**: Percentage-based overlap visualization
- **Set Membership**: Venn diagram style inclusion
- **Proximity**: Distance-based measurement
- **Identification**: Directional relationship from self to other
- **Projection**: Directional relationship from other to self
- **Attribution**: Attribute assignment between entities
- **Observation**: Observer perspective measurement
